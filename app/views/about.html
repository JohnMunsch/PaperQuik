FAQ

Why another graph paper web site?

Because almost all of the sites I went to were nothing more than a collection of PDF files for download. Some of them printed out OK, many didn't, and I usually couldn't do any customization of the page. Originally I wanted Cornell Note-taking System paper with a dot grid in the largest area on the page but I couldn't find any site with that available. So I've built what I think is a much better site than all the others.

May I put the paper on my website?

You're welcome to put links to my paper pages on your own site. I made sure the URLs will work if you want to link to the main page, a particular size of paper, or even a particular size and layout. But you can't download the images of pages or make PDF files from my pages and post them on your own site.
